Basic theory of ggplot

ggplot is based on the foundations in Leland Wilkinson’s book: The Grammar of Graphics.

It is built around the following principles:

  • Data
  • Aesthetics aes()
  • Statistics stat_
  • Scales scale_
  • Geometries geom_
  • Facets facet_
  • Coordinates coord_
  • Themes theme_
